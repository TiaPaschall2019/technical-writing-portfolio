# **How to Shampoo Your Hair**

1. Wet hair from root to tip.
2. Divide hair into sections so the scalp is easily accessible.
3. Place a quater-sized amount of shampoo directly onto the scalp in these sections.
4. Massage the shampoo *gently* into the scalp, then work shampoo through to the ends.
5. Rinse the shampoo completely and squeeze any excess water out before applying conditioner.
